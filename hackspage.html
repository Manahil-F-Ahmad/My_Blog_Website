General Hacks

Sesonal

    1. Check the highest and lowest temperatures.
    2. Pack clothes according to the temperatures.
    3. Pack your favorite hot/cold drinks.
    4. Book tours according to the weather.

Pakistan

    1. Wear modest clothes.
    2. If going in Summer:
    • Always have water.
    • Wear light clothes.
    • Take frequent breaks in the shade.
    3. If going in Winter:
    • Beware of landslides in the North.

State of Kuwait

    1. Ice cream is always the right answer.
    2. Water and juice are your best friends.
    3. Indoors over Outdoors in summer.
    4. Be Fasionable.
    5. The parks are amazing in winter.

Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

    1. Wear Modest clothes.
    2. Try out their snacks.
    3. The desert can get very cold in the nights, take appropriate clothes.
    4. Cold drinks are your best friends.

United Arab Emirates

    1. Visit cities other than Dubai.
    2. Fast food is amazing.
    3. Winter rains can cause short-term flooding.
    4. The rain mostly dries out in a few hours.

Bahrain

    1. At night, some beaches are very dark. Be Cautious.
    2. Summers are humid, pack accordingly.
    3. Visit the Souq for a cultural experience and souvenirs.

Qatar

    1. Stay Hydrated, the weather is dry.
    2. Try out Desert Safaries!
    3. Respect the local customs and traditions.
